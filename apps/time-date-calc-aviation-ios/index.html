Overview

Time & Date Calc - Aviation is the iOS edition of our time-and-date suite. Tools are grouped under four bottom tabs—Time Tools, Date Tools, Utilities, and Settings—matching the structure in the shipped app. Open a tab, choose a tool from the list, then work inside that screen’s navigation stack.

Calculations, history, records, countdowns, and preferences are stored on your device using Apple’s SwiftData and local settings; KavNik Technologies does not operate a backend that receives your calculator inputs or results. The free tier may show Google AdMob ads; Premium removes ads and lifts limits described below and in-app.

For the Android edition (Google Play), see Time & Date Calculator.

Navigation (as in the app)

  • Time Tools — Smart Time calc (add, subtract, multiply & divide time); Aviation forecast (time and date modes for component life and due dates); Fuel Calculator (trip fuel, reserves, average burn).
  • Date Tools — Date Calculator, Date Difference, Age Calculator, Workdays Calculator.
  • Utilities — Calculator (standard arithmetic), Time Zone Converter, Countdown Timer, Expense Splitter.
  • Settings — appearance (theme, accent), calculator preferences (default opening screen, haptics, sounds, decimal places, date/time format, first day of week), Premium, privacy and terms links, app version, rate/share, and feedback.

Core features

Smart Time calc

  • Time arithmetic with an in-app keypad; basic layout emphasizes add/subtract; full layout adds multiply and divide for time when you are a Premium user.
  • Toolbar actions typically include clear, save to Record, History, and backspace—exact icons follow the on-screen bar.
  • History and Records (with free-tier caps) mirror the Android product’s workflow for grouping sessions.

Aviation forecast

  • Segmented control at the top switches Time mode (hour-style fields such as AF/ENG hours, life expired, installed-at, due-at targets) and Date mode (manufacture vs installed anchors, interval in years/months/weeks/days, due-on date and life remaining).
  • Optional component name and tag for traceability; separate histories for time vs date forecasts where the app provides them.
  • Premium may unlock professional export where enabled in your build.

Fuel Calculator

  • Plan trip fuel with reserves and average burn—labels and outputs match the fields on device.
  • Uses the same history patterns as other tools where implemented.

Calculator (arithmetic)

  • Standard numeric calculator (not time units), with expression history and Records comparable to Smart Time calc.

Date Tools

Date Calculator, Date Difference, Age Calculator, and Workdays Calculator each maintain their own history; Age Calculator uses a tighter free-tier history cap in code (5 entries vs 10 for most other tools) unless Premium is active.

Time Zone Converter

  • Convert between regions/zones with local history for repeat conversions.

Countdown Timer

  • Multiple countdowns; uses notifications when you grant them. Free tier limits how many active incomplete timers you can have at once (5 in the current app logic).
  • Completed timer history is also capped for free users; Premium removes those caps.

Expense Splitter

  • Shared costs, trips, and settle-up style flows as implemented in-app (including navigation into a trip when you open one from the list).

History, records, and free-tier limits

Unless Premium is active, the app enforces caps aligned with the current Swift code: 10 history entries per calculator type (except 5 for Age Calculator), 10 records per supported tool, up to 5 active countdowns, and fair-use limits on CSV/PDF exports per month for free users. Premium unlocks unlimited retention (where applicable), export, full Smart Time layout, and removes ads—see Settings → Premium and the paywall for live pricing.

In-app purchases (StoreKit)

Purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. Product identifiers configured in the project include a default lifetime SKU com.KavNikTech.TimeCalc.pro_unlock (unless overridden by PRO_UNLOCK_PRODUCT_ID in Info.plist) and subscription IDs com.KavNikTech.TimeCalc.subscription.monthly and com.KavNikTech.TimeCalc.subscription.yearly when present. The exact offerings shown in-app depend on App Store Connect and your region.
