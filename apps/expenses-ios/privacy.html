Privacy Policy — Expenses (iOS)

Last updated: May 2026

iOS · iPhone & iPad

App overview Terms of use / EULA

Summary

Expenses (bundle com.kavniktech.expensesplitter) stores your ledgers, spaces, EMI plans, and related metadata primarily in SwiftData on device. KavNik Technologies Pvt Ltd does not operate our own cloud database that receives your expense rows as part of ordinary use.

If you enable iCloud sync, eligible data syncs through Apple CloudKit to the app’s private CloudKit database (iCloud.com.kavniktech.expensesplitter), subject to your Apple ID and iCloud settings. Personal travel content follows private-first sharing rules in the app.

Receipt images are stored as files under the app’s App Group container (group.com.kavniktech.expensesplitter) and referenced from models—not shipped as oversized inline blobs.

Optional exchange-rate hints (Frankfurter)

For non–base-currency flows, the app may request indicative rates from the public Frankfurter API (api.frankfurter.app). Those requests are typically limited to currency identifiers and dates needed for conversion hints; see Frankfurter’s documentation for their handling of logs or IP data.

Apple Intelligence / on-device models

AI Spending Insights features may use on-device processing and, where available, Apple’s frameworks for natural language and generation. Processing is governed by Apple’s system behavior and your device settings. KavNik does not operate a KavNik-owned server that stores your full Q&A transcripts for that flow.

Widgets and Siri / Shortcuts

The Home Screen widget reads rollup snapshot values the main app publishes into the App Group. Siri, Shortcuts, and App Intents run on device; phrases you configure may send structured fields you supply into the app.

Invites and deep links

Shared-space invites may use a custom URL scheme (expensesplitter://…). Opening a link launches the app locally; KavNik does not need to host invite payloads on KavNik servers for basic scheme handling.

In-app purchases

Subscriptions and lifetime unlock for Expense Tracker and Splitter Pro are processed by Apple StoreKit. Apple handles payment data. See Apple’s Privacy Policy for purchase-related processing.

Advertising

Some builds may include layout space reserved for optional third-party banners. If a shipped build shows ads, network providers may process device or interaction data under their policies—describe that accurately on the App Store and here. Do not rely on this page alone if the live product changes.

Permissions

Features such as receipt capture, voice entry, contacts-backed names, and Siri may request Camera, Photo Library, Contacts, Microphone, Speech Recognition, or related capabilities. Prompts and usage strings in the submitted Info.plist are authoritative.

Children’s privacy

The app is a general-purpose finance utility. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what Apple’s platforms, optional ads, or your own entries provide.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when features or regulations change. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.

Contact

Questions about this app policy: support@kavniktech.com or our contact page.

For the company website policy, see KavNik Technologies Privacy Policy.
